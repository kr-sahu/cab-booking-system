# Zuber Driver Portal

Welcome to the Zuber Driver Partner Portal. This section of the application is dedicated to our driver partners to manage their trips, profiles, and earnings.

## 🚀 Getting Started

To become a driver partner with Zuber, follow these steps:

1.  **Apply:** Visit the [Application Page](http://localhost/cab_app/driver/apply.php) and fill out your details, including KYC documents and license information.
2.  **Wait for Approval:** Our administration team will review your application. You can check your status by attempting to log in.
3.  **Login:** Once approved, use your registered email and password to access the [Driver Dashboard](http://localhost/cab_app/driver/login.php).

## 🛠 Features for Drivers

- **Real-time Trip Requests:** Receive and manage ride requests from customers.
- **Profile Management:** Update your personal information and profile picture.
- **Trip History:** View all your past completed trips and earnings.
- **Availability Toggle:** Switch between Online and Offline modes to control your work schedule.

## 🔐 Administrative Access

If you are a system administrator, you can manage driver applications and system settings via the Admin Panel:

**Admin Panel Login:** [http://localhost/cab_app/admin/login.php](http://localhost/cab_app/admin/login.php)
